Local Business & Freelancers (Normal Tax Regime)

Businesses selling locally, Freelancers with local clients, or those exporting physical goods are taxed on their Net Income (Gross Revenue minus Expenses) using progressive slabs.

Withholding Tax (WHT)
1% for Filers, 2% for Non-Filers deducted on bank receipts. This is adjustable against your final tax liability calculated below.

Freelancers / Export of Services

Income from international Service clients (IT, Consultancy, etc.) falls under the Final Tax Regime (FTR). You pay a flat % on Gross Earnings. No expense deductions allowed.
